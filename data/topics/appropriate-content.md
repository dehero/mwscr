# appropriate content

Original, previously unpublished screenshots and videos captured in [Morrowind](./morrowind.md) game, as well as
redrawings of previously published posts, photos, photomontages, out-of-game screenshots, and other materials related to
the project or the game are considered acceptable content. When talking about [post requests](./request.md) they should
be written clearly and don't contain vulgar language.
